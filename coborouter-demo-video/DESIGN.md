# CoboRouter Demo Video Design

## Style Prompt
CoboRouter should feel like a serious agentic finance control plane: dark, precise, auditable, and fast. The visual language is a dense technical dashboard with luminous proof signals, policy gates, and transaction traces. Use restrained motion, sharp hierarchy, and clear evidence labels rather than decorative spectacle.

## Colors
- `#08111F` base canvas, from the existing CoboRouter hero art.
- `#0D1829` elevated panels and console surfaces.
- `#38BDF8` agent/tool signal and active route trace.
- `#22C55E` approved wallet policy and successful proof states.
- `#F97316` settlement, transaction, and final receipt accents.
- `#F8FAFC` primary text; `#CBD5E1` secondary text.

## Typography
- Inter for product copy, labels, and captions.
- SFMono-Regular / Menlo / Monaco / Consolas for hashes, policy IDs, and transaction proof.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not make the wallet proof look like a generic crypto trading dashboard.
- Do not use fake provider/payment claims beyond the repo's receipt language.
- Do not hide the blocked path; the policy denial is a core feature.
- Do not use soft pastel, beige, or generic SaaS marketing styling.
- Do not overfill the frame with tiny JSON; show only judge-relevant proof.
